HOMEMATES

GA Project | 4 people team | 2 weeks

Homes.com is a resource for users looking to find nice homes they can buy or rent, but in a time where home sharing has become a solution for dealing with the rise in costs of renting and buying, users need trustworthy options that can help them find qualified roommates and properties to share.
Our solution is to build Homemates.com, a companion website to Homes.com that will transition the company into the sharing economy while revolutionizing the roommate discovery process to create meaningful living experiences.
