COOKING ASSISTANT

GA Project|Solo project|2 weeks

My first experience with UX Design was a GA project were I had to solve a day to day problem for a friend. I started my research by asking questions to my partner Han and I found that she wants to learn to cook her meals, but she needs easy recipes that can help her to save some time because she’s usually busy.
I decided to focus on the “Learning to cook easy recipes”, because if Han doesn’t have the idea of what she needs to do during the process, she will not even think about bigger problems (buying ingredients and tools, things for pros).
My solution: An App that will lead Han step by step with easy recipes for 1 to 4 servings, she will be able to schedule her meals and the extra features to order ingredients and cooking tools (Just for pros).
