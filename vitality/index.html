VITALITY

GA Project | 3 people team | 2.5 weeks

Peakfoqus creates smart solutions for peak health by taking advantage of Wearable Tech. Their current focus and inspiration are spirited seniors and patients.
We designed an app that enable caregivers to support seniors by monitoring their medication intake and setting appropriate reminders to assist in this goal.
The app will link up to a Smart Watch worn by the seniors, where they can receive reminders, messages and alerts.
